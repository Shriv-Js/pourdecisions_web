About us

Founded by two brothers and a lifelong friend, Pour Decisions aims to connect US retailers with wineries to create unparalleled, unique and exquisite private label wines. We work primarily with French wines and can also accommodate requests for Italian, Spanish, German and Argentinian wines.

Ilya Brown

Born in New York but raised in a small village nestled in the Provence area, Ilya quickly became in love with the French art de vivre, the food and of course the wine. Ilya always wanted to promote and bring some of this Provençal taste with him wherever he went. Having studied business and marketing in London, he felt the time has come to go back to his roots in the US, and develop a business that would encompass his passion for wine and branding. Ilya moved to Miami and immediately identified the growing need for hospitality establishments to distinguish themselves and strengthen their brand. 

Andrew Nivens:

Having grown up in between Virginia and New York, Andrew learned southern hospitality and gained New York energy. Having family from France as well many summers spent there, Andrew cultivated a love and passion for the wine heritage. Andrew now shares his time between the two continents and is able to share cultures and develop his passion, making it a dream come true. 

Chris Jones:

Son of British rock and roll legend Mick Jones, (leader and founder of super group Foreigner), Jones inherently developed a talent in the world of entertainment and hospitality. Going to High School with Andrew Nivens in New York City, and having nurtured Ilya Brown from a young age. Naturally the 3 entered into the wine business together. Growing up in the nightclub scene of NYC with mentors like Richie Akiva (Butter 1Oak Nomad Hotel), Jones moved to Miami to work with brother Roman Jones ( Kiki on the River, Gramercy, Santo) owning and running some of the number one clubs in the world. As part of the Opium Group, Jones hosted clubs such as Privé, Mansion, Opium Garden, Cameo, Set/Mr Jones and Mokai. Over the years, Chris Jones has appeared in numerous nationwide campaigns alongside Jay-Z for Rocawear and Diddy for Ciroc. Although, most recently known as a musician and member of Fat Joe's Terror Squad, Jones enthusiastically lives through Pour Decisions and is spicing up the wine game with his exclusive nightlife "expertise and flavor".
